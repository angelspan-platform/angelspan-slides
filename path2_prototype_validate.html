Path 2

Pilot + Validate

Prove the model works before committing full capital

Monthly spend rate — 16-month view
  • A working pilot is built using AI-assisted development tools with a small product and engineering team — not a full enterprise build*
  • The pilot is tested with a select group of existing clients, GPs, and LPs — real users, real data, intentionally limited scope
  • After the build, spend drops to ~$25k/mo — the team iterates based on real usage while early fund operations run on the pilot
  • At month 6–8, a decision point: if the model is validated, a professional engineering team builds the enterprise-grade platform — informed by everything learned in the pilot
  • Total spend to reach that decision: ~$200k — compared to ~$1M committed upfront in Path 1 before any real-world validation
  • Work produced in the pilot is not thrown away — it becomes the tested blueprint and design foundation for the full enterprise build

What the Pilot Covers — 3 Feature Areas*

1 — AngelSpan IR platformCore startup investor relations functionality on a modern foundation — profiles, update submission, PA review and curation, SMART IR scoring, stakeholder management, and billing. Improved account management and security (MFA, multi-user access) that the current platform lacks. Tested with a select group of existing clients before any broader rollout.
2 — Legacy Fund integrationThe first digital connection between a startup's AngelSpan data and the Legacy Fund layer. A startup can apply for funding through a portal, be screened for eligibility, and have their SMART IR scoring history inform the Champion review process. Builds on an AI-assisted interactive mockup already completed.
3 — Portfolio managementFund-level views for GPs, LPs, and Champions to monitor portfolio companies, track SMART IR performance, and manage fund activity. Designed to support Legacy Fund operations once capital is allocated — scope confirmed through real usage during the validation phase.
