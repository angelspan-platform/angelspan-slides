Monthly spend rate — 16-month view
  • Monthly investment ramps gradually to a peak of ~$150k/mo — the bulk of capital goes toward building the complete platform before anyone uses it
  • At launch, the platform is fully built: startup investor reporting, Legacy Fund management tools, and internal staff operations — all production-ready
  • No real users or revenue until month 9 — the full product is built based on what the team believes the market needs, not what the market has confirmed
  • After launch, monthly spend steps down through a stabilization period then settles at ~$18k/mo for ongoing engineering, support, and infrastructure
  • The full ~$1M is committed and spent before there is any evidence the business model works in practice
  • Market feedback — whether customers adopt, pay, and stay — doesn't arrive until month 12 or later
